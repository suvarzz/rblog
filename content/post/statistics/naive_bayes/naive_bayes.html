Naive Bayes

\(P(c|x) = \frac{P(x|c)(P(c))}{P(x)}\), where
\(P(c|x)\) - posteriour probability
\(P(x|c)\) - Likelihood
\(P(c)\) - Class Prior Probbility
\(P(x)\) - Predictor Prior Probability
